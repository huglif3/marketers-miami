WHY MEXICO CITY

01
The Builders
Are Already Here

Mexico City has one of the most active crypto and fintech ecosystems in LATAM. Etherfuse is building the rails for the Mexican peso stablecoin (MXNe) and tokenized CETES bonds on Stellar — cutting cross-border payment costs from 7% to under 0.04%.

Etherfuse · MXNe · CETES Stablebonds on Stellar
02
The Remittance
Capital of the World

Mexico received over $60 billion in remittances in 2024 — nearly 4% of GDP. For millions of families, cross-border money movement isn't a use case, it's survival. Stablecoins built on Stellar are cutting those costs in real-time. The marketers in this room are shaping how that story gets told.

#2 Global Remittance Recipient · $60B+ in 2024
