WHY MEXICO CITY

01
The Builders
Are Already Here

Mexico City has one of the most active crypto and fintech ecosystems in LATAM. Etherfuse is building the rails for the Mexican peso stablecoin (MXNe) and tokenized CETES bonds on Stellar — cutting cross-border payment costs from 7% to under 0.04%. This is where the infrastructure is being written. The room belongs here.

Etherfuse · MXNe · CETES Stablebonds on Stellar
02
Stacked Timing

The Stablecoin Conference LATAM runs June 15–16 in Mexico City — 2,500 attendees, 100+ speakers, 50+ countries, backed by Bitso, Circle, Mastercard, and Ripple. Marketers in the Arena on June 17th is the intimate, high-signal follow-up the conference audience actually wants. We show up when the builders are already in town.

Stablecoin Conference LATAM · June 15–16 · CDMX
03
Institutional Backing
& Collaboration

With support from and in collaboration with Catalaxia Ventures and Fundación Ricardo Salinas Pliego — bringing institutional weight, local reach, and a shared belief that the next generation of builders in LATAM deserves a room like this.

Catalaxia Ventures · Fundación Ricardo Salinas Pliego
04
The Remittance
Capital of the World

Mexico received over $60 billion in remittances in 2024 — nearly 4% of GDP. For millions of families, cross-border money movement is not a use case, it's survival. Stablecoins built on Stellar are cutting those costs in real-time. The marketers in this room are shaping how that story gets told.

#2 Global Remittance Recipient · $60B+ in 2024
